Travelling

I am a person interested in travelling along the western ghats region (tropical rainforest), partly influenced by the geography of Kerala, where I was born. I often get the feeling of being in close interaction with an unknown divine entity that helps me to free my mind and expose me to new ideas and thoughts. The experience and conversations with random people is something that I love in these trips. As Gustave Flaubert rightly said, "Travel makes one modest. You see what a tiny place you occupy in the world."
